About

Falooda is a rich and colorful dessert drink made with layers of falooda sev, basil seeds, rose syrup, milk, and topped with ice cream. Popular during summers and festivals, it's both cooling and indulgent.

Video

Steps

  1. Soak sabja seeds in water for 15-20 minutes until they swell
  2. Boil falooda sev and let it cool
  3. In a tall glass, add 1 tbsp rose syrup, soaked sabja, falooda sev, and a little sugar
  4. Pour chilled milk over it and top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream
  5. Garnish with chopped nuts and cherry; serve immediately
