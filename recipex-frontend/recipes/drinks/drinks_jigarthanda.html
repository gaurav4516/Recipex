About

Jigarthanda is a famous cold beverage originating from Madurai in Tamil Nadu. Known for its cooling properties, this rich and creamy drink is a delightful combination of milk, almond gum, nannari syrup, and ice cream, making it perfect for hot summer days.

Video

Steps

  1. Boil milk, reduce it slightly, and cool completely.
  2. In a glass, add soaked badam pisin and 1.5 tbsp nannari syrup.
  3. Pour chilled milk and condensed milk over it, stir well.
  4. Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and drizzle extra syrup if desired. Serve chilled.
