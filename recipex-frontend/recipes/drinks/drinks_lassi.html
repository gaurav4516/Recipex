About

Sweet Lassi is a traditional Punjabi yogurt-based drink, known for its refreshing and cooling qualities. It’s sweet, creamy, and flavored with cardamom and sometimes rose water, making it a perfect summer beverage.

Video

Steps

  1. Add curd, sugar, cardamom powder, and rose water in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy
  3. Add water and ice cubes, blend again
  4. Pour into glasses, garnish with chopped nuts, and serve chilled
