About

Jaljeera is a tangy and refreshing North Indian drink made with a blend of spices, herbs, and tamarind. It's a great digestive drink, commonly served as an appetizer during summers to cool the body and stimulate appetite.

Video

Steps

  1. Blend mint, coriander, tamarind pulp, and spices into a smooth paste
  2. Add the paste to 2 cups chilled water and mix well
  3. Serve with ice cubes and garnish with a pinch of roasted cumin powder
