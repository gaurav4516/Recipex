About

Nannari Sharbat is a traditional South Indian summer drink made from the roots of the Nannari plant (Indian Sarsaparilla). It’s known for its cooling properties and soothing taste, often served with lemon juice and ice during hot days.

Video

Steps

  1. Add 2 tbsp Nannari syrup and 1 tbsp lemon juice to each glass
  2. Fill the glass with chilled water and stir well
  3. Add ice cubes, mix again and serve immediately
