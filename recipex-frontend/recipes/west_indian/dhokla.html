About

Dhokla is a traditional Gujarati steamed savory cake made from fermented rice and chickpea batter. This fluffy, spongy snack is typically served with green chutney and tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves.

Video

Steps

  1. Mix besan, yogurt, turmeric, ginger-chili paste, sugar and lemon juice in bowl
  2. Add water to make smooth batter (pancake consistency)
  3. Let batter rest for 20 minutes
  4. Grease a steaming plate with oil
  5. Add Eno fruit salt to batter and mix gently
  6. Immediately pour batter into steaming plate
  7. Steam for 15-20 minutes in steamer
  8. Check doneness with toothpick
  9. Let cool for 5 minutes then cut into squares
  10. Heat oil in small pan for tempering
  11. Add mustard seeds, sesame seeds and curry leaves
  12. Pour tempering over dhokla and serve with chutney
