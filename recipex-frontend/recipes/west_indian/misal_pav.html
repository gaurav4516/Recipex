About

Misal Pav is a spicy Maharashtrian street food dish made from sprouted legumes cooked in a fiery gravy, topped with crunchy farsan and served with soft pav (bread rolls). It’s a filling and flavorful breakfast or snack.

Video

Steps

  1. Heat oil and add mustard seeds and curry leaves
  2. Add onions and sauté till golden brown
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute
  4. Add tomatoes and cook till soft
  5. Add red chili, turmeric, and goda masala
  6. Add boiled sprouts and mix well
  7. Add water to make a curry consistency and simmer for 10 mins
  8. Adjust salt and spice as needed
  9. Serve misal topped with farsan, chopped onions, coriander, and a wedge of lemon
  10. Serve hot with buttered pav
