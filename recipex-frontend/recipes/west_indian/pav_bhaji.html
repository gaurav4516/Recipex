About

Pav Bhaji is a iconic Mumbai street food consisting of spicy mashed vegetables (bhaji) served with buttered dinner rolls (pav). This flavorful dish is characterized by its rich tomato-based gravy and aromatic spices.

Video

Steps

  1. Pressure cook potatoes, peas and other vegetables until soft
  2. Mash the cooked vegetables thoroughly
  3. Heat 3 tbsp butter in a large pan
  4. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter
  5. Saute onions until golden brown
  6. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Mix in tomato puree and cook until oil separates
  8. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and pav bhaji masala
  9. Stir in mashed vegetables and mix well
  10. Add water to adjust consistency and simmer for 10 minutes
  11. Add lemon juice and coriander leaves
  12. Heat remaining butter in separate pan
  13. Toast pav buns in butter until golden
  14. Serve hot bhaji garnished with onions and lemon wedges
  15. Accompany with buttered pav and extra butter on top
