About

Bhelpuri is a beloved Indian street snack from Mumbai, made from puffed rice, veggies, chutneys, and crunchy sev. It’s a perfect mix of spicy, tangy, and sweet — usually enjoyed by the beach or roadside stalls.

Video

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix puffed rice, chopped onions, tomatoes, and boiled potatoes
  2. Add green chutney, tamarind chutney, lemon juice, and salt
  3. Sprinkle chaat masala and mix well
  4. Top with sev and chopped coriander
  5. Serve immediately to maintain crunch
