About

Poha is a popular Maharashtrian breakfast made with flattened rice, peanuts, and spices. This light yet satisfying dish is known for its perfect balance of flavors - slightly spicy, mildly tangy, and subtly sweet.

Video

Steps

  1. Rinse poha in water 2-3 times until soft. Drain completely
  2. Add turmeric, salt, and sugar to poha. Mix gently
  3. Heat oil in pan, add mustard seeds and let splutter
  4. Add peanuts and roast until golden
  5. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and sauté for 30 seconds
  6. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent
  7. Add diced potatoes and cook until tender
  8. Mix in prepared poha gently
  9. Cover and steam cook on low heat for 3-4 minutes
  10. Turn off heat, add lemon juice and coriander leaves
  11. Serve hot with sev and coconut garnishes
