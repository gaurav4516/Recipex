About

Vada Pav is Mumbai’s favorite street snack — a spicy potato fritter (vada) sandwiched between soft buns (pav), served with dry garlic chutney and fried green chilies. It’s filling, flavorful, and full of spice!

Video

Steps

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, then sauté garlic, green chilies, turmeric
  2. Add mashed potatoes and salt, mix well and cool
  3. Form small balls from the mixture
  4. Make a batter using besan, salt, water, and a pinch of baking soda
  5. Heat oil for deep frying
  6. Dip each potato ball in batter and deep fry until golden
  7. Slice pav buns, apply dry garlic chutney inside (optional)
  8. Place hot vada between pav, press gently
  9. Serve with fried green chilies and chutneys
