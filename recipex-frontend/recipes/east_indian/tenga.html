Ingredients

Rohu Fish

500g (cut into pieces)

Tomatoes

4 medium (chopped)

Outenga (Elephant Apple)

1 medium

Mustard Oil

5 tbsp

Turmeric Powder

1 tsp

About

Traditional Assamese sour fish curry cooked with elephant apple and tomatoes.

Video

Steps

  1. Marinate fish with ½ tsp turmeric and salt
  2. Heat mustard oil in earthen pot
  3. Fry fish pieces until golden. Set aside
  4. In same oil, add chopped tomatoes
  5. Add sliced elephant apple pieces
  6. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy
  7. Add 2 cups warm water
  8. Bring to boil and simmer 10 mins
  9. Add fried fish pieces gently
  10. Cook covered for 8-10 mins
  11. Add fresh coriander leaves
  12. Serve with steamed rice
