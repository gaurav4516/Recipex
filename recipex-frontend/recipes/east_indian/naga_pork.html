About

Traditional Tripuri pork curry with fermented bamboo shoots - A fiery delicacy from Northeast India.

Video

Steps

  1. Clean pork pieces thoroughly
  2. Boil pork with salt until tender
  3. Smash garlic and ginger into paste
  4. Heat mustard oil in earthen pot
  5. Add pork pieces. Fry until brown
  6. Add garlic-ginger paste. Sauté 5 mins
  7. Add sliced bamboo shoots
  8. Add chopped king chilies
  9. Cook on low heat for 45 mins
  10. Add water as needed
  11. Season with salt
  12. Serve with steamed rice
