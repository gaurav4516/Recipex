About

Traditional Mizo vegetable stew with fermented ingredients - A staple of Mizoram cuisine.

Video

Steps

  1. Clean and chop mustard leaves
  2. Boil bamboo shoots for 10 mins
  3. Pound garlic and chilies in mortar
  4. Heat water in earthen pot
  5. Add mustard leaves and bamboo shoots
  6. Add pounded chili-garlic paste
  7. Stir in fermented soybean
  8. Simmer for 25-30 mins
  9. Add salt to taste
  10. Serve with steamed rice
