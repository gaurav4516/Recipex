About

Traditional Odia fermented rice dish served with accompaniments - perfect summer coolant.

Video

Steps

  1. Take leftover cooked rice in clay pot
  2. Add water (1:1.5 ratio)
  3. Mix ½ cup curd. Stir well
  4. Cover & ferment for 8-12 hours
  5. Add sliced cucumber + green chili
  6. Sprinkle cumin powder. Serve chilled
