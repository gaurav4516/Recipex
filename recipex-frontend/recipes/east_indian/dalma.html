About

Traditional Odia lentil-vegetable curry cooked with indigenous spices and coconut.

Video

Steps

  1. Wash toor dal thoroughly
  2. Pressure cook dal with turmeric + 3 cups water (3 whistles)
  3. Heat mustard oil in kadai
  4. Add bay leaf + dry red chili
  5. Temper panch phoran (fenugreek, mustard, cumin, nigella, fennel)
  6. Add chopped vegetables: banana, pumpkin, potato, brinjal
  7. Sauté on medium heat for 5 mins
  8. Add cooked dal + 1 cup water
  9. Mix well and bring to boil
  10. Add salt + turmeric. Simmer 15 mins
  11. Prepare coconut paste: Blend coconut + cumin + garlic
  12. Add coconut paste to curry. Mix well
  13. Add jaggery (1 tbsp) for sweetness
  14. Cook for 5 more minutes
  15. Finish with ghee (1 tbsp)
  16. Garnish with coriander leaves
