About

Spongy cottage cheese balls soaked in light sugar syrup - Bengal's iconic sweet that's recognized as a GI-tagged culinary treasure.

Video

Steps

  1. Pour milk into heavy-bottom pan and bring to boil
  2. Add lemon juice gradually while stirring continuously
  3. Once milk curdles completely, strain through muslin cloth
  4. Rinse chenna under cold water to remove lemon flavor
  5. Hang chenna for 45 minutes to drain excess whey
  6. Knead chenna on smooth surface for 15 minutes
  7. Make smooth balls (1.5" diameter) without cracks
  8. Prepare sugar syrup: Boil sugar + water + cardamom
  9. Gently drop balls into boiling syrup
  10. Pressure cook for 10 minutes (without weight)
  11. Let pressure release naturally
  12. Chill in refrigerator for 4 hours
  13. Serve cold with syrup
