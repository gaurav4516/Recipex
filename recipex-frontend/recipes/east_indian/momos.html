About

Soft steamed dumplings filled with spiced chicken - A Himalayan delicacy loved across India.

Video

Steps

  1. Make dough: Mix maida + ¾ cup water + pinch salt. Knead 10 mins
  2. Cover dough with damp cloth. Rest 30 mins
  3. Prepare filling: Mix chicken + onions + garlic + ginger + soy sauce
  4. Add black pepper + salt. Mix well
  5. Divide dough into 20 equal balls
  6. Roll each ball into 4" diameter circle
  7. Place 1 tbsp filling in center
  8. Pleat edges to seal - make 5-6 folds
  9. Press pleats tightly to seal momo
  10. Grease steamer plate with oil
  11. Arrange momos leaving space between them
  12. Steam in bamboo steamer for 15 mins
  13. Prepare chutney: Blend tomatoes + chili + garlic + salt
  14. Heat oil in pan. Add cumin seeds
  15. Pour blended mixture. Cook 5 mins
  16. Serve hot momos with tomato chutney
