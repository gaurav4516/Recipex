Ingredients

1 cup

Agar powder

2 tsp

Sugar

1/2 cup

Water

1 cup

Optional: Matcha powder (for green yokan)

1 tsp

About

Yokan is a traditional Japanese sweet jelly made from red bean paste, agar agar, and sugar. It has a smooth, firm texture and is often sliced and served with tea.

Video

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, dissolve agar powder in water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add sugar and stir until completely dissolved.
  3. Mix in the red bean paste (and matcha powder if using) and cook until smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture into a mold and let it cool to set.
  5. Once firm, slice into pieces and serve with green tea.
