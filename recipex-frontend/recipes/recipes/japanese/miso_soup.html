Japanese Miso Soup

Serves

4 persons

Ingredients

Dashi stock

4 cups

Miso paste (white or red)

3 tbsp

Soft tofu

1/2 block, cubed

Wakame seaweed (dried)

2 tbsp, soaked

Green onions

2 stalks, sliced

About

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup made from fermented soybean paste (miso), dashi broth, tofu, and seaweed. It's a staple in Japanese meals, known for its comforting and umami-rich flavor.

Video

Steps

  1. Bring the dashi stock to a simmer in a pot over medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve the miso paste with a ladle of hot dashi broth.
  3. Reduce heat to low and slowly add the miso mixture back into the pot without boiling.
  4. Add the cubed tofu and soaked wakame seaweed; heat gently for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat, garnish with sliced green onions, and serve immediately.
