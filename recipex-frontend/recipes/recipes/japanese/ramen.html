About

Delicious Japanese ramen with rich chicken broth, tender chicken slices, noodles, and flavorful toppings.

Video

Steps

  1. Prepare chicken broth by simmering chicken bones and vegetables
  2. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions
  3. Slice cooked chicken breast thinly
  4. Soft boil eggs and peel them
  5. Combine broth and soy sauce in a pot and heat
  6. Place noodles in bowls, pour broth over noodles
  7. Add chicken slices, eggs, bamboo shoots, and green onions on top
  8. Garnish with seaweed sheets and serve hot
