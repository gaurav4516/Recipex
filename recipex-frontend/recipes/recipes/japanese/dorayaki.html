About

Dorayaki is a popular Japanese confection consisting of two fluffy pancakes sandwiched with sweet red bean paste filling. Itâ€™s a beloved traditional snack, perfect with tea or as a dessert.

Video

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until smooth. Add honey and water, mix well.
  2. Sift flour and baking powder into the mixture, stir gently until just combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat. Pour small rounds of batter (about 3-4 inches in diameter).
  4. Cook pancakes until bubbles form on surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Let pancakes cool slightly. Take one pancake, spread a generous layer of sweet red bean paste on the flat side.
  6. Top with another pancake to form a sandwich, gently press to seal.
  7. Serve immediately or wrap and store for later. Enjoy!
