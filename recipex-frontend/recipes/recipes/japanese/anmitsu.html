About

Anmitsu is a traditional Japanese dessert featuring agar jelly cubes, sweet red beans, fresh fruits, and mochi balls, typically drizzled with brown sugar syrup. It's a refreshing and colorful treat, especially popular in summer.

Video

Steps

  1. Prepare agar agar jelly cubes according to package instructions and let them set.
  2. Arrange agar cubes in serving bowls.
  3. Add a spoonful of sweet red bean paste (anko) on top.
  4. Add fresh mixed fruits and shiratama dango (mochi balls) around the jelly.
  5. Pour kuromitsu (brown sugar syrup) over everything.
  6. Add ice cubes if desired and serve immediately for a refreshing dessert.
