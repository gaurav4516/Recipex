About

Classic Japanese sushi rolls made with fresh salmon, avocado, and cucumber wrapped in seaweed and seasoned rice.

Video

Steps

  1. Rinse sushi rice until water runs clear
  2. Cook rice with 1 1/4 cups water
  3. Mix rice vinegar into cooked rice; cool to room temperature
  4. Place nori sheet on bamboo mat
  5. Spread rice evenly over nori, leaving top edge clean
  6. Arrange salmon, cucumber, and avocado in a line on rice
  7. Roll tightly using bamboo mat
  8. Slice roll into bite-sized pieces
  9. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi
  10. Enjoy your homemade sushi!
