About

Japanese tempura is a classic dish of seafood and vegetables lightly battered and deep-fried to crispy perfection.

Video

Steps

  1. Prepare shrimp and vegetables by cleaning and slicing
  2. Mix tempura batter mix with cold water; do not overmix
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to 170°C (340°F)
  4. Dip shrimp and vegetables into batter
  5. Deep fry until golden and crispy, about 2-3 minutes
  6. Drain excess oil on paper towels
  7. Serve hot with dipping sauce
  8. Enjoy your homemade tempura!
