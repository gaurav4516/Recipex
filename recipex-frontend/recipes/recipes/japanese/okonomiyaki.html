About

Okonomiyaki is a savory Japanese pancake filled with cabbage, topped with pork belly, and drizzled with tangy sauces and bonito flakes. It's often called "Japanese pizza" and is a popular street food and home-cooked dish.

Video

Steps

  1. Mix okonomiyaki flour with water or dashi to make a smooth batter.
  2. Add shredded cabbage and eggs to the batter and mix well.
  3. Heat a griddle or large pan over medium heat and lightly oil it.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and shape into a round pancake.
  5. Place pork belly slices on top of the pancake.
  6. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, then carefully flip and cook the other side until pork is cooked through.
  7. Transfer to a plate and top with okonomiyaki sauce, Japanese mayo, bonito flakes, and aonori.
