About

Taiyaki is a popular Japanese fish-shaped cake traditionally filled with sweet red bean paste. Crispy on the outside and soft inside, it's a beloved street snack across Japan.

Video

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and sugar.
  2. Beat the egg and add to the mixture along with milk, stirring until smooth.
  3. Heat a taiyaki pan or a fish-shaped mold and grease it lightly with oil.
  4. Pour some batter into the mold, then add a spoonful of red bean paste in the center.
  5. Cover the filling with more batter and close the mold.
  6. Cook on medium heat for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Remove from the mold and let cool slightly before serving.
