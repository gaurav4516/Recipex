Ingredients

Sweet red bean paste (anko)

1 cup (for filling)

About

Daifuku is a traditional Japanese sweet made of soft and chewy mochi stuffed with sweet red bean paste. Itâ€™s a popular snack and dessert, loved for its chewy texture and sweet filling.

Video

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, mix glutinous rice flour and sugar.
  2. Add water gradually and stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave the mixture for 2 minutes, stir, then microwave again for 1-2 minutes until thick and sticky.
  4. Dust a clean surface with potato starch and transfer the mochi dough onto it.
  5. Divide the dough into 8 equal portions and flatten each.
  6. Place a spoonful of sweet red bean paste in the center, wrap the mochi dough around it, and seal gently.
  7. Coat the finished daifuku with more starch to prevent sticking. Serve fresh.
