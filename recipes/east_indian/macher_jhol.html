About

Light Bengali fish curry with potatoes in mustard oil - A quintessential comfort food from Bengal.

Video

Steps

  1. Clean fish pieces thoroughly. Pat dry
  2. Marinate fish with ½ tsp turmeric + ½ tsp salt
  3. Heat 3 tbsp mustard oil in pan until smoking
  4. Shallow fry fish 2 mins per side. Set aside
  5. In same oil, add ½ tsp kalonji (nigella seeds)
  6. Add bay leaf + 1 dry red chili
  7. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds. Let splutter
  8. Add 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste. Sauté 1 min
  9. Add chopped tomatoes + 1 tsp turmeric
  10. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy (5 mins)
  11. Add potato pieces + ½ tsp red chili powder
  12. Pour 2 cups hot water. Bring to boil
  13. Simmer until potatoes are 80% cooked
  14. Gently add fried fish pieces
  15. Add 4 slit green chilies
  16. Cook covered on low heat for 8-10 mins
  17. Finish with 1 tbsp mustard oil + coriander
  18. Serve hot with steamed rice
