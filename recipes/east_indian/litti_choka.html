About

Traditional Bihari dish featuring roasted wheat balls with spiced sattu filling, served with mashed vegetable chokha.

Video

Steps

  1. Make dough: Mix wheat flour + ajwain + salt + 1 tbsp oil + water
  2. Knead into firm dough. Rest 30 mins
  3. Prepare sattu filling: Mix sattu + chopped onion + garlic + green chili + lemon juice
  4. Add mustard oil + mango powder + salt. Mix well
  5. Divide dough into 12 equal portions
  6. Flatten each portion into 3" circle
  7. Stuff 2 tbsp sattu mixture. Seal tightly
  8. Shape into round balls. Ensure no cracks
  9. Preheat oven to 200°C (392°F)
  10. Bake littis for 25-30 mins until golden
  11. Alternatively, roast on coal for authentic flavor
  12. Prepare baingan chokha: Roast brinjal on open flame
  13. Peel and mash roasted brinjal
  14. Boil potatoes. Peel and mash
  15. Mix mashed veggies + chopped onion + green chili
  16. Season with mustard oil + salt + coriander
  17. Prepare tomato chutney: Roast tomatoes + garlic
  18. Mash and mix with mustard oil + salt
  19. Dip hot littis in ghee before serving
  20. Serve with chokha and chutney
