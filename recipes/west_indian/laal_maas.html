About

Laal Maas is a fiery Rajasthani mutton curry, traditionally cooked with mathania red chilies and aromatic spices. Known for its bold flavors and vibrant red color, it's a royal delicacy often enjoyed with bajra roti or rice.

Video

Steps

  1. Soak red chilies in warm water for 15 minutes, grind into a paste
  2. Heat mustard oil until it reaches smoking point, then cool slightly
  3. Add whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon) and sauté for 30 seconds
  4. Add sliced onions and sauté till golden brown
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste, sauté till raw smell goes
  6. Add red chili paste and cook for 3–4 minutes
  7. Add mutton pieces, cook on high flame for 5–6 minutes till browned
  8. Lower the flame, add whisked curd slowly while stirring continuously
  9. Cook on medium flame for 10 minutes
  10. Add water as required, cover and cook till mutton becomes tender (40–50 mins)
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander (optional), serve hot with rice or roti
