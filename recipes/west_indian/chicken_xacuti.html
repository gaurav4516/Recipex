About

Chicken Xacuti is a traditional Goan curry made by roasting aromatic spices and coconut, then simmering it with chicken for a deep, rich, and spicy flavor profile. It’s typically served with rice or pav.

Video

Steps

  1. Dry roast coconut, poppy seeds, red chilies, coriander seeds, peppercorns, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamom till aromatic
  2. Grind the roasted ingredients into a smooth paste with water
  3. Heat oil and sauté onions till golden brown
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for 1–2 minutes
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  6. Add the ground spice paste and turmeric, fry till oil separates
  7. Add chicken pieces and coat well with the masala
  8. Add water as needed, cover and cook until chicken is tender
  9. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or pav
